# 🚚 Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📖 Overview

The **Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI solution designed to provide comprehensive visibility into supply chain operations. The dashboard enables businesses to monitor logistics performance, supplier contributions, transportation efficiency, route optimization, and product quality metrics through intuitive visualizations and KPIs.

This project demonstrates how Business Intelligence can be leveraged to transform raw operational data into actionable insights for strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall supply chain performance.
- Track revenue, profit, and operational KPIs.
- Analyze supplier performance and contributions.
- Evaluate transportation mode effectiveness.
- Identify route optimization opportunities.
- Monitor product quality through defect analysis.
- Support data-driven supply chain decisions.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Product Performance
- Skincare products generated the highest revenue.
- Product-level defect analysis highlights quality trends.
- Product category comparisons support inventory planning.

### Transportation Analysis
- Rail transportation contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Transportation mode analysis helps identify cost-efficient logistics strategies.

### Supplier Performance
- Supplier-wise revenue analysis identifies top-performing suppliers.
- Supports supplier evaluation and procurement optimization.

### Quality Monitoring
- Defect rate tracking provides visibility into product quality.
- Inspection outcome analysis helps improve operational standards.

### Route Optimization
- Route cost versus revenue analysis helps optimize logistics operations.
- Customer demographic insights support demand planning and resource allocation.
